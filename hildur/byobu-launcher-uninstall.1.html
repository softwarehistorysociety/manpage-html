NAME

byobu-launcher-uninstall - Byobu Launcher uninstallation utility

SYNOPSIS

byobu-launcher-uninstall

DESCRIPTION

byobu-launcher-uninstall(1) is a simple utilty that removes the line added to your $HOME/.profile by byobu-launcher-install(1) which launches byobu(1) any time you login through ssh(1) or on a console(4).

SEE ALSO

screen(1), byobu(1), ssh(1), console(4), byobu-launcher-install(1)
http://byobu.org
